    Bio

    Hello world! I am a third year Ph.D. student in economics at UC San Diego.

    Grown up in Brianza, I studied economics in Milan (Bocconi) and Victoria B.C. (UVic), worked as an investment consultant in Phnom Penh, then went back to Bocconi to be an RA and TA, and finally, moved to San Diego for my doctoral studies.

    Research Interest: Macroeconomics, Fiscal and Monetary Policy, Production Networks, Macroeconometrics.
