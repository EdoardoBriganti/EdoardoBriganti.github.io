Edoardo Briganti

Bank of Canada

I am a senior economist at the Bank of Canada. I work in the Economic and Financial Research (EFR) and Canadian Economic Analysis (CEA) departments.

I received my PhD in Economics from UC San Diego in June 2024.

Research Interests: Macroeconomics, Fiscal and Monetary Policy, Production Networks, Public Economics.
